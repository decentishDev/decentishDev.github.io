Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9th, 2023


This Privacy Policy describes how https://decentishdev.github.io/ (referred to as "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our website and any games or other software we have created and promoted.


Website Information Collection

We do not collect any personal data of any kind from you on our website, and we do not store any data on your device.

However, our website contains links to other websites that may collect and store your data. We do not have any control over these third-party websites, and their privacy policy along with the ways they collect and store data is listed on those specific websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these websites before providing any personal information.

We may collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, and device information for statistical purposes. We may also collect information about your use of our website, including the pages you visit and the time spent on each page. This information is used to improve our website and to better understand how our visitors use our website.


Games and Software Information Collection

Any games or other software we have created and promoted may collect data such as the number of downloads, daily players, and ads run. We use Unity Analytics, a third-party service provided by Unity Technologies, to collect this data. Unity Analytics collects this data through the use of cookies and similar technologies. Unity Technologies' privacy policy can be viewed at https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy.

The games are also hosted and downloaded by external third-party sites which may collect other data. These third-party sites may also use cookies and similar technologies to collect data. We do not have any control over these third-party sites, and their privacy policies along with the ways they collect and store data are listed on those specific sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these sites before providing any personal information.

We may also collect non-personal information about your use of our games and software, including the time spent playing the game, the levels completed, and the in-game items purchased. This information is used solely for statistical purposes and to improve our games and software.


Use of Information

The non-personal information we collect is used solely for statistical purposes and to improve our website and games.


Sharing of Information

We do not share any personal information with any third parties. We may share non-personal information with third-party service providers to help us improve our website and games and to better understand how our visitors use our website and games.

We may also share a small portion of statistics with third-party websites, such as the number of people who have viewed their website. However, we are only able to view their data that has to do with us or the content we've uploaded to these third-party websites.


Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted on this page.


Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at decentishdev@gmail.com.
