SNAZZY SLOPE

Snazzy Slope is an infinite runner game based upon rushing down a neon universe and avoiding the many obstacles that stand in your path, all fueled by the bright lights and energizing synthwave. Steer your vessel by tapping on either side of the screen. Avoid red obstacles and jump between platforms to survive as long as possible. Collect orbs along your journey to purchase skins and equip extra-life tokens. Download now (for free!!!!) to experience the infinite world of Snazzy Slope.
